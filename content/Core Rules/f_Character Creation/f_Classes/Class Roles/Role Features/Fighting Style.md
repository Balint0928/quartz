When you gain this feature, you can choose an option from below. If you would gain this feature from multiple sources, you can't choose the same fighting style more than once.

- **Volley Style:** You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls you make with bows over 30ft away

- **Puncturing Shots:** You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls you make with Crossbows against enemies within 30ft

- **Defense:** While you are wearing Armor, your armor's AV increases by 1.

- **Dueling Style:** When you are wielding a Dueling Weapon in one hand and no other Weapons, you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls with that Weapon.

- **Great Weapon Fighting:** When you roll a 1 or 2 on a damage die for an attack you make with a Melee Weapon that has Heft (6) or greater, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll, even if the new roll is a 1 or a 2.

- **Protection:** When a creature you can see attacks a target other than you that is within 5 feet of you, you can use your Reaction to add your Shield's AV to the target's DV.

- **Thrown Weapon Fighting:** You can draw a weapon that has the Thrown property as part of the attack you make with the Weapon. In addition, when you hit with a ranged attack using a Thrown Weapon, you gain a +2 bonus to the damage roll.

- **Two-Weapon Fighting:** Attacks with your off hand (has to be wielding two light weapons) are used when you make your attack action and no longer require a bonus Actions to use.

- **Provocateur:** While a creature that attacks you is under the effect of your provoke, you can make an Intimidation, Persuasion or Performance check against the creatureâ€™s passive Insight. On a success, the effect of your Provoke is extended for another round.

- **Brawler:** When you hit with your Unarmed Strike and deal damage, you can deal Bludgeoning damage equal to 1d4 plus your Strength modifier instead of the normal damage of an Unarmed Strike. When you reach level 5, the d4 becomes a d6. Additionally, at the start of each of your turns, you can deal 1d4 Bludgeoning damage to one creature Grappled by you.
