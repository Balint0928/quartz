You gain the following features if your character possesses the wanderer role at the indicated levels:

**Level 1:**
- Skill Proficiency, Expertise (1 skill), [[Fleet of Foot]]

**Level 2:**
- [[Endurance]]

**Level 3:**
- [[Skill Mastery]]

**Level 4:**
- [[Class feat]] or [[Role feat]]

**Level 5:**
- [[Skill Mastery]]
